Frequently Asked Questions

Isn't This Just Spam? +

No. Spam blasts a message out to everyone without consideration. Liftlio connects with only those who could benefit from your solution.

Here's the difference:
❌ Spam: "Check out my product! [link]"
✅ Liftlio: "I had the same issue. [Specific context about their problem]. Here's what worked for me: [your solution with context]."

How is this different from just posting about my product myself? +

You could spend hours daily hunting for relevant conversations. Or you could ship features while we do it automatically, at scale, with better targeting than you'd do manually.

Will this get me banned or flagged as spam? +

We only comment where genuinely relevant in a human-sounding way. The mentions are contextual and helpful—the kind you'd make yourself if you had unlimited time.

Can I pause or cancel anytime? +

Yes. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. No long-term commitment.
